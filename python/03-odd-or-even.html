Odd or Even

Is it odd or even?

Beginner

The script asks you for a number and tells you whether it's odd or even.

Enter a number: 3
You habe entered an odd number!
Enter a number: 4
You have entered an even number! 
#!/usr/bin/python3
# 03-odd-or-even.py

while True:

    number_input = int(input("Enter a number: "))
    number = number_input % 2

    if number == 0:
        print("You have entered an even number!")
    else:

        print("You habe entered an odd number!")
